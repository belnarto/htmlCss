• JPG image format is great for photos and images with lots of gradients in them. On the other hand, JPGs don’t allow for transparent pixels.
  • GIFs are good for animations.
  • PNGs are great for anything that’s not a photo or animated, like diagrams, icons, etc.
  • SVGs are vector based. Use them wherever you can.
